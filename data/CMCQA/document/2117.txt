The table contains information about a disease called dental pulp disease. The symptoms of this disease include tooth sensitivity to hot and cold, pain when biting, erosion of tooth enamel, widening of the gap between teeth, and toothache. The diagnosis of this disease can be done through oral X-ray examination and oral endoscopy. The recommended drugs for treatment are oral solution of acetaminophen and capsules of artificial musk and metronidazole.
