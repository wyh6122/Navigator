The CSV contains information on a disease, specifically Coronary atherosclerosis. The symptoms of the disease include sharp chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, chest tightness, palpitations, irregular heartbeat, lymphedema, leg cramps or spasms, and burning chest pain. Medical tests that can be conducted to diagnose the disease are hematologic tests (blood test), electrocardiogram, lipid panel, glucose measurement (glucose level), ultrasonography (ultrasound), echocardiography, and hemoglobin A1c measurement (hemoglobin a1c test). Medications that can be prescribed for the disease include aspirin, metoprolol, clopidogrel (Plavix), simvastatin, nitroglycerin, carvedilol, isosorbide, rosuvastatin (Crestor), digoxin, ezetimibe (Zetia), and ramipril.
