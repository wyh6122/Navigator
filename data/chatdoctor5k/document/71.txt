The csv contains information about Endophthalmitis, including common symptoms such as pain in the eye, redness of the eye, and diminished vision. Medical tests that may be performed include other therapeutic procedures on eyelids, conjunctiva, and cornea, as well as ophthalmic examinations and evaluations. Medications that may be used for treatment include Erythromycin, Bacitracin Ophthalmic, and Tetracaine.
