The csv contains information about a disease called Pilonidal cyst. The symptoms of this disease include skin growth, lower body pain, ache all over, skin swelling, pain of the anus, bones are painful, back mass or lump, mass or swelling around the anus, pelvic pain, fluid retention, irregular appearing scalp. Medical tests that can be performed to diagnose this disease are wound care management, complete physical skin exam performed, incision and drainage, excision (removal), rectal examination, culture wound, and other OR therapeutic procedures on skin and breast. The medications that can be used to treat this disease are Clindamycin, Miconazole Topical Product, Betamethasone-Clotrimazole Topical, Silver Nitrate Topical Each, Magnesium Sulfate, Quinidine, Clavulanate (Augmentin), Salsalate, Sodium Hypochlorite Topical, Isosorbide Dinitrate, Linezolid (Zyvox), and Dicloxacillin.
