The csv contains information about a disease called Restless Leg Syndrome. The symptoms of this disease include abnormal involuntary movements, leg pain, headache, sleepiness, apnea, fatigue, abnormal breathing sounds, depression, back pain, difficulty breathing, loss of sensation, and insomnia. Medical tests that may be used to diagnose this disease include other diagnostic procedures (interview; evaluation; consultation), lipid panel, hemoglobin A1c measurement (Hemoglobin a1c test), other therapeutic procedures on eyelids; conjunctiva; cornea, magnetic resonance imaging (Mri), examination of foot, and depression screen (Depression screening). Medications that may be prescribed to treat this disease include Ropinirole, Pramipexole (Mirapex), Gabapentin, Modafinil (Provigil), Tizanidine, Rizatriptan (Maxalt), Sitagliptin (Januvia), Ramelteon (Rozerem), Primidone, Doxepin, and Solifenacin (Vesicare).
