The csv contains information about a disease called Peritonitis. The symptoms of this disease include sharp abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, back pain, fever, shortness of breath, lower abdominal pain, upper abdominal pain, side pain, groin mass, intermenstrual bleeding, and groin pain. Medical tests that can be used to diagnose this disease include Hematologic tests (Blood test), Radiographic imaging procedure, Complete blood count (Cbc), Intravenous fluid replacement, X-ray computed tomography (Scan ct), Kidney function tests (Kidney function test), and Glucose measurement (Glucose level). The medications that can be used to treat this disease include Metronidazole, Fentanyl, Vancomycin, Ceftazidime, Zosyn, Cinacalcet (Sensipar), Rocuronium, Cefoxitin, Iohexol (Omnipaque), Diatrizoate, and Sennosides, Usp (Perdiem).
