The given dataset contains information related to a disease called Uterine fibroids. The symptoms associated with this disease include sharp abdominal pain, pelvic pain, heavy menstrual flow, lower abdominal pain, unpredictable menstruation, painful menstruation, intermenstrual bleeding, long menstrual periods, infertility, vaginal discharge, involuntary urination, and cramps and spasms. Medical tests that can be conducted for diagnosis include pelvis exam, radiographic imaging procedure, complete blood count (Cbc), urinalysis, ultrasonography (Ultrasound), examination of breast, and standard pregnancy test. Treatment for this disease can involve medications like Medroxyprogesterone, Leuprolide (Lupron), Progesterone, Clomiphene (Clomid), Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (Hcg), Norethindrone (Loestrin Fe), Sevoflurane, Nafarelin (Synarel), Butoconazole Topical, Goserelin (Zoladex), and Ferrous Sulfate.
