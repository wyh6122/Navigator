The csv contains information about a disease called Infectious gastroenteritis. The symptoms of the disease include vomiting, diarrhea, nausea, sharp abdominal pain, fever, burning abdominal pain, headache, blood in stool, decreased appetite, chills, fluid retention, and flu-like syndrome. Medical tests used to diagnose the disease include hematologic tests (blood test), complete blood count (Cbc), intravenous fluid replacement, urinalysis, kidney function tests (Kidney function test), electrolytes panel, and glucose measurement (Glucose level). Medications that can be used to treat the disease include Metronidazole, Loperamide (Imodium), Trimethobenzamide (Tigan), Bismuth Subsalicylate (Pepto-Bismol), Lactobacillus Acidophilus And Bulgaricus, Sodium Citrate, Sodium Hypochlorite Topical, Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine), Pepsin A, and Betamethasone.
