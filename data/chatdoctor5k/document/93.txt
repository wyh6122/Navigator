The csv contains information on Chronic Sinusitis. Symptoms of Chronic Sinusitis include cough, nasal congestion, headache, sore throat, fever, frontal headache, coryza, ear pain, painful sinuses, sinus congestion, facial pain, and congestion in chest. Medical tests that can be conducted to diagnose Chronic Sinusitis include X-ray computed tomography (Scan ct), other diagnostic procedures (interview; evaluation; consultation), diagnostic procedures on nose; mouth and pharynx, influenzavirus antibody assay, other diagnostic procedures on skin and subcutaneous tissue, other OR therapeutic procedures on nose; mouth and pharynx, and ophthalmologic and otologic diagnosis and treatment. Medications that can be used to treat Chronic Sinusitis include Augmentin, Mometasone Nasal Product, Guaifenesin (Mucinex), Clarithromycin, Cefdinir, Pseudoephedrine (Pcm-La), Cefuroxime, Azelastine Nasal, Triamcinolone Nasal Product, and Oxymetazoline Nasal.
