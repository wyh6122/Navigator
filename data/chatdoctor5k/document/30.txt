The csv contains information on a disease, specifically Cirrhosis. Symptoms of the disease include sharp abdominal pain, shortness of breath, peripheral edema, fatigue, upper abdominal pain, abdominal distention, stomach bloating, blood in stool, regurgitation, symptoms of the kidneys, vomiting blood, and melena. Medical tests that can be used to diagnose the disease include hematologic tests (blood test), complete blood count (CBC), glucose measurement (glucose level), electrolytes panel, lipid panel, liver function tests (liver test), and hemoglobin A1c measurement (hemoglobin A1c test). Medications that can be used to treat the disease include furosemide, spironolactone, lactulose, tacrolimus (Prograf), nadolol, mycophenolate mofetil (Cellcept), propranolol, thiamine, methadone, ursodiol (Urso), epoetin alfa (Procrit), and peginterferon alfa-2B (Sylatron).
