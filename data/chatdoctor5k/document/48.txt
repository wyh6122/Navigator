The csv contains medical information about Teething syndrome. Symptoms of the syndrome include fever, pulling at ears, cough, irritability, nasal congestion, diarrhea, ear pain, gum pain, symptoms of infants, temper problems, redness in ear, and decreased appetite. Medical tests that can be performed include a complete physical skin exam, other diagnostic procedures such as interviews, evaluations, and consultations, and an ophthalmic examination and evaluation. Recommended medications for Teething syndrome include Rotavirus Vaccines, Pneumococcal 7-Valent Conjugate Vaccine, Benzocaine Topical, Mumps Vaccine, Sodium Citrate, Measles Vaccine, Rubella Virus Vaccine, Purified Protein Derivative Of Tuberculin, and Cholestyramine Resin.
