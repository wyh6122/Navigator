The csv contains information on a disease called Emphysema. Its symptoms include sharp chest pain, shortness of breath, chest tightness, cough, nausea, ache all over, back pain, cross-eyed, itchy eyelid, excessive growth, emotional symptoms, and elbow cramps or spasms. Medical tests that can be performed to diagnose the disease include radiographic imaging procedure, plain x-ray (X ray), complete blood count (Cbc), hematologic tests (Blood test), x-ray computed tomography (Scan ct), and kidney function tests (Kidney function test), and intravenous fluid replacement. Medications that can be used to treat Emphysema include Sodium Chloride (Deep Sea), Diphenhydramine (Benadryl), Hydromorphone (Dilaudid), Sucralfate (Carafate), Nicotine, Zosyn, Epinephrine, Budesonide, Adderall, Losartan, Combivent, and Penicillin.
