The dataset contains information about a disease called Cysticercosis. Symptoms of this disease include seizures, headache, vomiting, shortness of breath, abnormal involuntary movements, elbow cramps or spasms, elbow weakness, excessive growth, being underweight, low back weakness, and wrist weakness. To diagnose this disease, medical tests such as radiographic imaging procedures, hematologic tests (blood tests), complete blood count (CBC), X-ray computed tomography (scan CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), glucose measurement (glucose level), and CAT scan of head (head CT) are recommended. Treatment options for Cysticercosis include medications such as phenytoin (Dilantin), levetiracetam (Keppra), calcium carbonate, metoclopramide, albendazole, desipramine, magnesium sulfate, nortriptyline, carbamazepine, folic acid, and mecamylamine.
