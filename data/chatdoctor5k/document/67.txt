The given csv contains information about the disease Pulmonary eosinophilia. The symptoms of this disease include cough, shortness of breath, fever, nasal congestion, sharp chest pain, difficulty breathing, dizziness, ache all over, nosebleed, redness in ear, fluid retention, and paresthesia. Medical tests that can be done to diagnose this disease are radiographic imaging procedure, plain x-ray, hematologic tests such as complete blood count, intravenous fluid replacement, electrolytes panel, and kidney function tests. Some medications that can be prescribed to treat this disease are ceftriaxone, benzonatate, moxifloxacin (Avelox), prednisolone, lactase, ubiquinone (Q10), primidone, and candesartan (Atacand).
